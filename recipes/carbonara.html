Carbonara

Spaghetti carbonara, a classic Italian dish

Another well known but classic dish, this tasty dish is incredibly simple to make and tastes superb.

Ingredients

  • Spaghettti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti
  2. Cook the pancetta
  3. Add the garlic
  4. Combine the spaghetti and pancetta
  5. Add egg yoke
