Enchiladas

A mexican dish that is a favourite around the world.

The combination of tomatoes, chilli peppers and cheese makes this a comforting and filling dish, and can be combined with most types of meat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chilli peppers
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Wraps

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Reserve 1/3 of the sauce
  4. Combine the sauce and chicken
  5. Add this combination into the wraps
  6. Pour over reserved sauce and top with cheese
